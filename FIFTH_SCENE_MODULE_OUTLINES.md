# Fifth Scene Module Outlines

## Purpose

This document converts the fifth-scene research and module-structure planning
into writing-ready module outlines.

It is still a planning artifact, not the final scene script. The goal is to
define:

- what each module is trying to teach
- how the 10 questions in each module should be distributed
- what kind of result-card direction each module should eventually support

## Master Scene

Working scene title:

- `智慧農場全球任務站`

Master flow:

1. shared introduction
2. module selection
3. one locked 10-question run
4. module-specific result outcome

## 1. 技術題組

### Module Role

- 智慧農場技術探索員

### Module Intent

This module should answer:

- what devices and systems make smart farming possible
- how data is collected and used in field operations
- why sensing, imaging, and AI matter together

### 10-Question Topic Distribution

1. what makes a farm "smart"
2. soil / climate / environment sensing basics
3. weather-station or dashboard quick decision
4. image recognition and crop monitoring
5. drone inspection use case
6. AI prediction and alerts
7. matching devices to functions
8. ordering a basic smart-farm data flow
9. system integration question
10. why this technology matters beyond convenience

### Result Direction

Suggested result roles:

- `感測部署觀察員`
- `田間數據判讀員`
- `農場系統整合手`
- `智慧農務技術策士`

## 2. 商業題組

### Module Role

- 智慧農業經營規劃員

### Module Intent

This module should answer:

- why farms adopt smart agriculture in the first place
- what kinds of cost, ROI, and labour pressures matter
- why technical value and business value are not always the same thing

### 10-Question Topic Distribution

1. why smart agriculture becomes attractive to farms
2. labour shortage and aging workforce
3. ROI and payback basic concept
4. premium crops versus large-scale crops
5. farm investment branching decision
6. traceability as business value
7. postharvest or supply chain logic
8. adoption barrier and cost-risk trade-off
9. which investment brings value fastest
10. why commercial reality matters for agritech success

### Result Direction

Suggested result roles:

- `農場投資判讀員`
- `智慧營運協調員`
- `農業價值鏈分析手`
- `智慧農業導入策劃者`

## Recommended First Implementation Priority

If the user wants to implement modules step by step, I recommend this order:

1. 智慧灌溉題組
2. 技術題組
3. 商業題組
4. 水資源治理題組
5. 能源題組
6. ESG 題組
7. 國際市場題組

Reasoning:

- 智慧灌溉題組 is the clearest bridge between technology, water, and visible
  smart-farm behavior
- 技術題組 is the easiest companion module after that
- 商業題組 helps widen the scene into adoption reality
- the remaining modules can then deepen governance, sustainability, and
  international perspective
